About 4k Heights:

Welcome to 4k Heights where you can track your progress towards completing Appalachian Mountain Club's (AMC) New Hampshire 4,000 footer list! A 4,000 footer is a mountain that has an elevation of at least 4,000 feet and a minimum of 200 feet prominence.

With 4k Heights you can document your progress towards completing AMC's 4,000 footer list. you can also see trail conditions, routes, and more!
